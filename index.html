01 / 07   Instrument Ney Turkey & Persia

The ancient reed flute, the voice of the soul in Sufi tradition. Cut from cane at the river's edge, its breathy tone carries seven centuries of longing across every note.

02 / 07   Instrument Oud Morocco & Middle East

The king of instruments. Ancestor of the European lute, its pear-shaped body resonates with the harmonics of court music from Andalusia to Baghdad.

06 / 07   Instrument Erhu China

Two strings, infinite expression. The Erhu's horsehair bow draws from its silk strings a voice of such warmth and intimacy that it has been called the Chinese violin's weeping soul.

07 / 07   Instrument Flamenco Guitar Spain

Fire and melancholy in one instrument. Where Moorish legacy meets Iberian passion — the flamenco guitar carries eight centuries of Andalusian memory in every rasgueado.
